Our Philosophy

Where design
meets soul

House of Interior is a full-service interior design and fit-out studio rooted in Delhi NCR. We believe extraordinary spaces are born at the intersection of vision, craft, and precision — not from templates, but from listening deeply to how you live and work.

From conceptual ideation to final handover, our multidisciplinary team manages every layer of transformation — structural, aesthetic, functional — under one roof. Whether a luxury residence, corporate HQ, or bespoke furniture commission, we bring the same reverence for detail to every brief.

What We Do

Our Core Services

Four disciplines, one vision — delivering spaces that are as functional as they are unforgettable across Delhi NCR.
01
Interior Designing
Comprehensive spatial design — mood boarding, material curation, and detailed working drawings. Every element calibrated for beauty and function.
02
Turnkey Projects
End-to-end execution — civil, MEP, joinery, finishes, and handover. One point of accountability, transparent timelines, zero compromise.
03
Modular Furniture
Factory-precision modular systems for kitchens, wardrobes, and workstations. Engineered for longevity, finished for elegance.
04
Soft Furnishing
Drapes, upholstery, rugs, and textiles — the tactile layer that transforms a space from designed to lived-in. Sourced globally, curated locally.
